# Prompt Iterations

## Iteration 1

### Prompt
Create a short advertisement for a local café.

### Output
The generated advertisement was informative but lacked emotional appeal and a strong call-to-action.

### Observation
The content needed more authenticity and a better social media style.

---

## Iteration 2

### Prompt
Create a 30-second UGC-style advertisement for Brew Haven Café targeting students and professionals.

### Improvements
- More conversational tone
- Better audience targeting
- Clear benefits
- Stronger CTA

---

## Iteration 3

### Prompt
Act as a professional UGC creator.

Generate an Instagram Reel advertisement including:
- Hook
- Problem
- Solution
- Product Experience
- Emotional Benefit
- CTA

### Result
The generated content became much more engaging, natural, and suitable for social media marketing.

---

## Learning

Prompt refinement significantly improved:
- Advertisement quality
- Audience engagement
- Marketing effectiveness
- Content structure
- Conversion potential
